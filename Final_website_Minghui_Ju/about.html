Minghui Ju Jason
Born and raised in Jiangsu, China. I moved to New York in 2015 and studied and worked there for 5 years. Currently I am in Shanghai for the Tongji Global Offsite program operated by Parsons School of Design. Minghui Ju is my Chinese name and I go by Jason.
About me
I really enjoyed playing with the products in my life and I chose industrial design as my major during my undergraduate studies. During my studies at Pratt, I learned not only about the combination of form, color and function of objects, but also about the history of products, which was particularly important. After that, I was keen to combine design with technology and wanted to make pioneering designs. Therefore, I chose MFA DT at Parsons School of Design as my next stop.
